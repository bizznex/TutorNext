# BizzNexx Prototype Ontology

## Core Objects

### Business

The service business BizzNexx supports.

### Catalogue

The public offer structure of the business. It includes services, proof, FAQs, CTAs, and the promise shown to prospects.

### Catalogue Item

One service, class, package, workshop, or consultation offer.

### Trust Proof

Testimonials, results, certifications, portfolio examples, or proof that builds buyer confidence.

### Inquiry

A form submission or WhatsApp click from the public page.

### Lead

A qualified inquiry that needs follow-up, trial scheduling, fee discussion, or conversion.

### Trial Class

A proposed, confirmed, completed, missed, or rescheduled trial.

### Customer

A converted student/client.

### Payment

A fee, advance, balance, or monthly due.

### Follow Up

The next action BizzNexx recommends.

### AI Message

A generated or templated message for WhatsApp or follow-up.

### Growth Score

Measures catalogue readiness, inquiry capture, trust proof, response speed, and follow-up health.

### Health Score

Measures operational discipline: follow-up, conversion, collection, documentation, and repeat customer health.
